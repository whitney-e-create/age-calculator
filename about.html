About This Website

This Age Calculator was created to provide a simple, fast, and accurate way to calculate your age in years, months, and days. No personal data is stored or required — just enter your date of birth and get your result instantly.

Why We Built It

We noticed that many online calculators were slow, overloaded with ads, or required extra clicks. Our goal is to keep things clean, modern, and easy for everyone.

Who We Are

This project was developed by Whitney E. as a learning and micro‑business experiment. We plan to expand with more everyday tools like date difference calculators and converters.
